ABOUT US

Andiwo Pizza Palace might be one of the most recognisable icons of Kibera.
Whichever way you slice it, Andiwo Pizza Palace is an industry leader
in the quick service pizza game. The 1rst Andiwo Pizza Palace
outlet opened its doors in 2018 in Kibera, Nairobi, and rose quickly
in popularity. Serving hot oven-fresh pizza with its signature
all-natural tomato sauce and using only the freshest ingredients,
it has established itself at the top of the industry and is easily
one of the first choices in quick service pizza in Kenya.
